Features

Everything you need to retire with confidence

Monte Carlo Simulation

Thousands of stochastic paths using real historical NASDAQ, Fed Funds, and CPI data from 1926 to present.

Historical Backtesting

Replay against 2008, dot-com crash, 1970s stagflation, and 2020 COVID shock — real market periods, real results.

Month-by-Month Cash Flow

Not annual averages — precise monthly simulation tracking every account, withdrawal, and income source.

Pension Tracking with COLA

Model fixed pension income with accurate cost-of-living adjustments across any time horizon.

Mortgage Modeling

Track home value appreciation, loan amortization, and home equity as part of your complete retirement picture.

Priority Withdrawal Strategies

Control which accounts are tapped first for tax efficiency. Six strategies including proportional, rebalancing, and high-return-first.

Under the Hood

Analysis     Survivorship probability · Max sustainable withdrawal (binary search)
Strategies   Priority order · Moving average · High-return first · Proportional · Rebalance
