How It Works

The intelligence loop

Every thought you capture sets four things in motion — automatically.

1

Capture

Any text, URL, or idea. No templates, no categories required.

2

Enrich

AI auto-extracts type, topics, people, and action items in seconds.

3

Connect

Vector embeddings map your thought against your entire knowledge base.

4

Surface

News alerts, location briefings, and calendar prep — pushed to you proactively.

Semantic Search

Search by meaning, not keywords

Ask "what have I thought about authentication?" and retrieve everything you've captured about login, identity, OAuth, or Sign in with Apple — even if you never used those exact words.

HNSW vector indexing with 1536-dimensional embeddings means search is instant, and results reflect what you actually meant.

Features

A complete personal intelligence system

Semantic Vector Search

1536-dimensional embeddings with HNSW index. Find thoughts by meaning, not by keyword.

Auto Metadata Extraction

AI reads every capture and pulls out type, topics, people, action items, and dates — instantly.

Proactive News Alerts

Monitors Google News RSS for the exact topics in your thought library. Relevant articles come to you.

Location Intelligence

Geofencing triggers relevant tasks and notes when you arrive at a location. No GPS drain — significant location changes only.

Claude AI Chat

Conversational AI with full access to your thought library. Ask questions, find patterns, get briefings.

MCP Server for Claude

One config entry gives Claude Desktop full access to search and capture in your Minder knowledge base.

Auth         Stateless HMAC-SHA256
